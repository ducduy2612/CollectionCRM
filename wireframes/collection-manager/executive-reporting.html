Collection Department | Generated: May 5, 2025

Executive Summary

The Collection Department achieved a recovery rate of 48.5% in April 2025, exceeding the target of 47% and showing a 2.5% improvement over the previous month. Total collections reached $3.2M, representing a 14% increase from March. Cost to collect ratio improved to 13.3%, down from 14% in March, resulting in an overall ROI of 7.5:1.

Key achievements include the successful implementation of the Digital-First Approach strategy, which has shown promising early results with a 42.8% recovery rate at only 2.1% cost ratio. The Call Center Team continues to be the top performer with a 9.6:1 ROI.

Areas requiring attention include the Late Stage Team performance, which declined for the third consecutive month, and resource constraints in the Digital Collections Team, which is currently understaffed relative to demand.
